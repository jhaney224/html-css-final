Best Practices

There are important tips that should be considered for enhanced SEO.

Title Tag

  • Keep your titles at 60 characters or less to ensure they don’t appear clipped by search engines.
  • Keep one title tag per page and place it in the <head> section to make sure search engines know which page they should rank. Be sure to also ensure they are unique to each page and differ from your competitors.
  • Have a clean title for a professional look (eg. capitalizing the first letter in your title and proofreading it for spelling and grammar).

Meta Descriptions

  • The ideal length for a meta description is 120-158 characters (enough for mobile devices to see it but not too long for Google to display).
  • Sometimes less is more. Getting right to the point, with the most targeted and important information within the first 120 characters can increase your CTR (click-through rate).
  • Keep your meta descriptions catchy, inviting, descriptive, and thoughtful while still being concise.
  • Include your keyword where it makes sense. The best keyword to use is a word or phrase searched frequently by your target audience, is relevant to your page, and is already within your site’s ranking power.
  • Have unique meta descriptions for each page on your site. By doing this, your page can cast a wider net and incorporate various targeted keywords to drive more users.

Other Main Tips

Targetted Keywords
If your tags include frequently searched keywords, it’ll more likely boost both your visibility and your organic traffic. Google can highlight matched keywords in the search result to show their relevance.
A Strong Call-To-Action
Create a sense of urgency to get more clicks and make sure it’s specific to your page.
Unique Voice
Having unique descriptions allows you to reach more users while using targeted keywords. Do this by having unique descriptions on each page of your site.
